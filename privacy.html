Privacy Policy

Elle's Assistant — an internal tool of Five & Done. Last updated 25 June 2026.

Elle's Assistant ("the app") is a private, internal tool used by Five & Done staff to help draft and publish Jira tickets and track time. Access is restricted to a fixed allow-list of company email addresses; the app is not offered to the general public.

What we store

When you sign in and use the app, we store the following:

  • Sign-in identity — your Google account email address and display name.
  • Authorization tokens — Google and (if you connect Jira) Atlassian OAuth access/refresh tokens, plus your Atlassian Cloud ID. These are held in the app's server-side session store and are used only to call Google and Jira APIs on your behalf.
  • Time-tracking data — time-grid entries you enter, stored against your email address.

What we do not store

  • We do not store your Atlassian Account ID.
  • We do not copy or retain Jira issue content or other people's personal data from Jira.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

How your data is used

Stored data is used solely to operate the app for you: keeping you signed in, creating and updating Jira tickets under your own account when you connect Jira, accessing your Google Calendar where applicable, and saving your time entries.

Retention

Session and token data persists for the life of your session and is removed when the session expires or you sign out / disconnect Jira. Time-grid entries are retained until deleted by an administrator. You may request deletion of your data at any time using the contact below.

Third-party services

The app integrates with Google (sign-in and Calendar) and Atlassian Jira. Your use of those services is also governed by their respective privacy policies.

Contact

For any questions about this policy or to request deletion of your data, contact edefrain@fiveanddone.com.
