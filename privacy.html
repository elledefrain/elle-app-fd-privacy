Privacy Policy

Elle's Assistant — a personal project by Elle-Marie DeFrain. Last updated 25 June 2026.

Independent personal project. Elle's Assistant ("the app") is an independent tool built and operated personally by Elle-Marie DeFrain. It is not a product of, affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated on behalf of any employer or organization. Each person who uses it connects their own accounts and acts only with their own existing access.

The app is a private utility that helps the people invited to it draft and publish their own Jira tickets and keep simple time notes. Access is limited to a fixed list of invited email addresses; the app is not offered to the general public.

What we store

When you sign in and use the app, the following is stored:

  • Sign-in identity — your Google account email address and display name.
  • Authorization tokens — Google and (if you connect Jira) Atlassian OAuth access/refresh tokens, plus your Atlassian Cloud ID. These are held in the app's server-side session store and are used only to call Google and Jira APIs on your behalf, as you direct.
  • Time notes — time-tracking entries you enter, stored against your email address.

What we do not store

  • No Atlassian Account ID.
  • No Jira issue content or other people's personal data pulled from Jira.
  • Your data is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties.

How your data is used

Stored data is used solely to operate the app for you: keeping you signed in, creating and updating Jira tickets under your own account when you connect Jira, accessing your Google Calendar where applicable, and saving your time notes. The app acts only within the access your own accounts already grant.

Retention

Session and token data persists for the life of your session and is removed when the session expires or you sign out / disconnect Jira. Time notes are retained until deleted. You may request deletion of your data at any time using the contact below.

Third-party services

The app integrates with Google (sign-in and Calendar) and Atlassian Jira. Your use of those services is also governed by their own respective privacy policies and terms.

Contact

For any questions about this policy or to request deletion of your data, contact edefrain@fiveanddone.com.
